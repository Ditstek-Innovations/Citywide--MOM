**Outcomes:**

### 2. Team Structure and Communication
**Discussion Points:**

- Defined escalation paths for technical and business issues

**Outcomes:**
- Communication charter documented and accepted by both teams
- Slack channel #acmecorp-portal created

### 3. Technical Architecture Discussion
**Discussion Points:**
- Reviewed current system architecture and infrastructure
- Discussed technology stack: React frontend, Node.js backend, PostgreSQL database
- Security requirements including OAuth 2.0 and data encryption
- Performance targets: page load under 2 seconds, 99.9% uptime

**Outcomes:**
- Architecture review scheduled for December 17
- Michael Chen to prepare detailed technical design document
